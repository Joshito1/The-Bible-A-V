  • What do other websites consist of for storing data for front end.
    • - Most websites use a combination of local storage, session storage, and cookies to store data on the front end.
    • - Some websites also use IndexedDB for more complex data storage needs.
    • - Additionally, many websites utilize APIs to fetch and store data on the server side.
    • - Some websites may also use third-party services like Firebase or AWS Amplify for data storage and management.
